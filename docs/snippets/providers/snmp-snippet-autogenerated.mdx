## Authentication
This provider requires authentication.
- **listen_port**: UDP port to listen for SNMP traps (required: True, sensitive: False)
- **listen_address**: IP address to bind to (required: False, sensitive: False)
- **community_string**: SNMPv1/v2c community string (required: False, sensitive: True)

Certain scopes may be required to perform specific actions or queries via the provider. Below is a summary of relevant scopes and their use cases:
- **receive_traps**: Receive SNMP traps on the configured UDP port. (mandatory)
