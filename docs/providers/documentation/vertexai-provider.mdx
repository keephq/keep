## Connecting with the Provider

1. In the [Google Cloud Console](https://console.cloud.google.com/), open **IAM & Admin > Service Accounts**.
2. Create (or select) a service account and grant it the **Vertex AI User** role (`roles/aiplatform.user`).
3. Generate a JSON key for the service account and paste its **full contents** into the `service_account_json` field.
4. Set `project_id` to your GCP project ID and `region` to the region where your endpoints live (default `us-central1`).

## What Keep Monitors

Keep polls all Vertex AI online-prediction **endpoints** in your project and surfaces:

- Deployed models whose `healthState` is `UNHEALTHY` or `DEGRADED`
- Endpoints that have no deployed models at all

Healthy endpoints are silently ignored.
